AIF

White Dove, with its extensive experience in the financial markets, specializes in guiding high-net-worth individuals toward Alternative Investment Funds (AIFs). Regulated by SEBI, AIFs are privately pooled investment vehicles designed for sophisticated investors seeking portfolio diversification.

We offer expert advice on the three AIF categories: Category I, which supports startups and SMEs driving economic and social growth; Category II, focusing on private equity and debt funds for steady growth; and Category III, comprising hedge funds for high-risk, high-reward strategies. With a minimum investment requirement of ₹1 crore, we ensure that our clients understand the potential of AIFs to foster long-term capital growth in underserved sectors, aligning these investments with their financial goals and aspirations.
